An advancement in NFT technology.

NFTSIG brings verifiable and superimposed signature technology to any NFT. Through this breakthrough, anyone is able to sign any NFT in a quick and simple method. In addition, NFTSIG is a platform which provides significant utility to holders through its unique burn mechanism.

NFTYSIG Vault

To mint a signable NFT a user must first purchase NFTYSIG tokens (which are burnt upon minting) and aquire their desired NFT. At launch we will have an inagural phase where each unique signable link will cost a fixed 100 NFTYSIG to generate - however this cost will gradually increase through governance proposals in the NFTYSIG DAO. When a NFTYSIG NFT is minted, the original NFT is locked up in a NFTYSIG Vault and in order to prevent the market with being flooded with derivatives of a given NFT, it will cost 10x the minting cost to unstake and receive the original NFT back.

Intelligent Signing

NFTSIG uses three main methods to ensure the trustworthiness of the ecosytem. First, metadata from the original NFT is captured in any signed NFTYSIG NFT. This ensures that clones cannot simply be signed and sold as they will have different metadata upon inspection. Secondly, each NFT signing event generates a one time link that can be used by the receipient to sign an NFT. Lastly, all NFTs can be verifiably signed through any of the verified methods such as selfie and voice imposing, twitter verification, and even on chain wallet signing. One unique aspect of NFTSIG is that signed NFTs can be signed themselves and through this method its possible for an NFT such as an NBA Topshot NFT to have the signatures of the entire basketball team!

Token Incentives

NFTSIG's NSIG token is a governance token which allows the community to decide its future utility and the utility of the NFTSIG ecosystem. Proposals could include increasing the signing costs of NFT's to even adding a tax on every NSIG transaction. At launch, each signing of an NFT will lead to NSIG tokens being burnt - thus increasing the value of each individual NSIG token.
